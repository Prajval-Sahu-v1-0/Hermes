What is Hermes?

A Discovery Tool, Not an Agent

Hermes helps you find the right creators to collaborate with—without the noise, the guesswork, or the endless scrolling.

Smart Discovery

Describe the kind of creator you're looking for, and Hermes generates targeted search queries to surface the best matches across YouTube.

Qualitative Insights

Instead of raw numbers, you see human-readable labels like "High engagement" or "Recently active"—so you understand the creator at a glance.

Your Search, Your Control

Hermes doesn't make decisions for you. It presents ranked results based on your criteria. You decide who to reach out to.

Hermes is a tool, not an agent.

We don't negotiate, manage, or represent creators. Hermes is designed to streamline your search and surface relevant creators—everything else is up to you.
